Travel & Accommodation

Practical tips for flights, visas, transport, weather & more.

Flights & Airports

  • Most convenient airport for Hanoi: Noi Bai International Airport (HAN).
  • Other major airports: Tân Sơn Nhất (SGN) in Ho Chi Minh City & Da Nang (DAD).
  • Recommended domestic airlines: Vietjet Air, Bamboo Airways, Vietnam Airlines.

Visa Requirements

  • Most visitors require a visa; many nationalities can get an e-Visa (up to 90 days).
  • Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond your stay.
  • Check the latest rules for your specific passport type well in advance.
  • Official Info: Vietnamese Embassy, Vietnam Visa
  • German Travel Advice: Auswärtiges Amt

Getting Around

  • Grab App: Best for ride-hailing (cars & motorbikes).
  • Taxis: Use companies like G7 or Mai Linh (use meter).
  • Public Transport: Buses and minivans are affordable.
  • By Foot/Cyclo: Great for exploring city centers.
  • Motorbike Rental: Only for EXPERIENCED riders with an international driver's license.

Weather & Currency

  • Weather in October: Generally pleasant, ~25–30°C; Central region can be rainy.
  • Currency: Vietnamese Dong (VND).
  • Payments: ATMs are common and cards widely accepted; carry cash for small vendors.
  • Exchange Rate Guide: 1 EUR ≈ 30,000 VND.

Health & Safety

  • Ensure standard vaccinations are up-to-date; consider Hepatitis A/B, Typhus, Rabies.
  • Use mosquito protection to prevent dengue.
  • Drink only bottled or filtered water.
  • Emergency: Police (113), Fire (114), Ambulance (115).
  • Important: Get comprehensive travel insurance with repatriation.

Recommended Apps & Tools

  • Airalo/Sally: Easy eSIMs for data.
  • Grab: Transport & food delivery.
  • Google Translate: Download Vietnamese offline.
  • Maps.me / Google Maps: Navigation.
  • XE Currency: Live exchange rates.
